NavBar Example 2

This example extends on the NavBar1 example by showing and hiding sections below the nav.

Importantly, the sections are encapsulated and can't be directly modified. In order to have the nav items change which sections are shown or hidden, we dispatch a custom event from the nav items.

The body component listens for the custom 'opensection' event and shows the appropriate section. Because the sections are children of the body tag (always a bind-component), the body can show or hide the sections.
